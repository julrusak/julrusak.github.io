Hello! I'm Julianna Rusakiewicz.
A Theater Artist, Developer and Yogi based in New York City.
As an energetic artist and developer, I aspire to create visually dynamic and functional technologies. As a designer for the stage, I developed an eye for visual communication, which has served me well as a programmer. I enjoy both the collaborative process and working solo on projects and am enticed by large problems. I've learned to stop asking myself, "What do I want to do?" and start asking myself, "What problem do I want to solve?"
