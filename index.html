Hello, I am Julianna currently based in New York.

Theatre artist turned software engineer, I spend my free time cooking and devising low-waste recipes, teaching or practicing yoga, and training for my next marathon. Before my career change into tech, I taught media arts throughout New York City public schools. I believe anyone has the ability to learn any subject if it is broken down into digestible parts. I believe everyone is doing the best they can with the tools they have and I aspire to help as many as I can develop their toolbox.
