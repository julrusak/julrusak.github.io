Welcome! I'm Julianna Rusakiewicz.
A Theater Artist, Developer and Yogi based in New York City.
ABOUT
I moved to New York City to be an actress. Along the way I discovered directing then lighting and media design. I spent my twenties working in the theater scene when I met some designers who were coding their projections. One thing led to another and I found myself at Dev Bootcamp, learning web development.
I love making functional and beautiful technologies. I love solving problems. I love a challenge. I began working as a developer and couldn't cope sitting all day so I returned to a daily yoga practice, something I focused on during my acting career.
Yearning for a greater challenge and a deeper practice, I enrolled in a 200 hour Teacher Training this upcoming fall. I never thought I would be where I am, so it's safe to say I don't know where I'm going. Then again, do any of us?
