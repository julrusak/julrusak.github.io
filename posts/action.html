Take Action
September 6, 2016

“Action is movement with intelligence. The world is filled with movement. What the world needs is more conscious movement, more action.” ~ B.K.S. Iyengar

I was having a conversation with a friend last night about life in one's twenties and how to make the most of that decade. It's a weird decade of life, your twenties, you're becomming an adult for real, not just legally, and you're navigating relationships and increasing responsibility while continuing to develop mentally. Your interests are changing and not always at the same pace as your friends, so it can be a confusing and upsetting time.

I had this realization that I may have used yoga as a distraction, unknowingly at the time, but as I look back, I think my love for yoga began when I dedicated that time to myself, just a few hours a week, to shut out the rest of the world. That time was mine to look inward and not plan events or do work but to just be. To be present. And in doing so, I became a more conscious person, more aware.

And I'm still working on my awareness. I consider the weight of my words and how my experiences, and lack thereof, inform my opinions and actions. So, take action! Start by meeting yourself!
