Preparing for the Beginning!
September 13, 2016

YTT begins this Friday and I am so excited! I am preparing myself for weekends full of philosophy and practice. I am preparing to be indoors a little bit more throughout this fall. I am preparing to focus more attention inward and journal my growth. I am preparing for the unkown.

In doing so, I began to wonder about the ritual of preparation. It is back to school season, after all, so it's appropriate that I, too, am heading back into a learning environment. I picked out a new notebook, and bought a new pair of yoga pants. It seems that when we begin something new we feel that externally we need to represent that with new material things. After I bought a new yoga mat I started thinking about how I need an assortment of new things, but I don't. I don't need anything material. I need to be present. I need to make time for practice and reading. That's it, really.

So I'm trying, this school year, to prepare for a new journey without too much new stuff. I mean, I already bought the yoga mat and I love it, so....
