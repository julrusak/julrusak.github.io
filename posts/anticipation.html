I'm So Excited (and I just can't hide it)!
September 15, 2016

Tomorrow is day ONE of YTT!! I'm so excited to start this journey. I'm so excited for all the upcoming change in life. I'm excited to practice and meditate everyday. I have set another intention of rising early each morning to have some quiet time to myself.

I love this feeling of anticipation. I love that moment before something begins and I start imagining what it will be like. I do this all the time, for better or worse. Tomorrow kicks off with an opening ceremony, and while it will most likely be some meditation and setting intentions, I'd like to think it will be more of an Olympic style passing the torch ceremony. Either way, I'll be sure to write all about it for Saturday. Stay tuned!
