Keeping it Real!
September 14, 2016

I hopped up on a rock wall and started thinking of all the poses I could do, then I thought for a moment. The wall was narrow, and the drop on either side was just enough to do some minor damage. I contemplated but ultimately knew that I couldn't guarantee my safety. So I hopped down. Look, I'm journaling my way through this process and taking pictures along the way, but let's keep it real. I'm not about to break an arm for a cool yoga photo. Know what you're capable of and let it drive you. I will go back to that rock wall when I'm confident in my strength and take some beautiful photos of my journey.

It's the same as knowing when your body isn't feeling some things. Some days I practice handstands for hours. Some days I don't feel like practicing inversions at all. Some days I drop dough at the farmer's market. Some days I don't feel like eating vegetables, yesterday was one of those days. Every single meal I had was white, brown, or red. Eggs, bread, quinoa pasta, sauce, chicken, yogurt, granola, an apple, a latte, popcorn. Not one green. Not one single vegetable. Just keepin' it real.

Some days I'm at the yoga studio by 7:30am. Some days I wake up and stay in child's pose on my mat for twenty minutes. Just keepin' it real. I try my best. It's all I can do.
