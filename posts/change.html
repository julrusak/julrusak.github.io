Embracing Change
September 7, 2016

I ended the summer with a short vacation before preparing for the start of my 200 hour Yoga Teacher Training. With my studies will come the change into my favorite season, among other changes. Pumpkin everything will commence, sweaters will be pulled out from the closet, and all my favorite boots will make their season debut. To say I am excited is an understatemnt.

I feel sad for people who fear change. I understand their hesitance and I wish there was something that could be done, but without change how can one grow? What is something in your control that you wish you could change? The bigger question is, what's holding you back?
