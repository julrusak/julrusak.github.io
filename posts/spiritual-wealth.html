Spiritual Wealth
August 31, 2016

"As animals, we walk the earth. As bearers of divine essence, we are among the stars. As human beings, we are caught in the middle, seeking to reconcile the paradox of how to make our way upon earth while striving for something more permanent and more profound."
~ B.K.S. Iyengar

From the moment I saw the Sri Yantra symbol, I knew I wanted it on my body, permanently. I read somewhere that it is the symbol for 'fullness of breath', which spoke to me - living life in that moment where one is fully prepared for any adventure - but I've yet to find actual documentation of that meaning. What I have found is something equally as beautiful.

"The Sri Yantra is believed to bring about material & spiritual wealth, strengthen relationships, enhance peace, happiness, empowerment, abundance & an alignment with the Universal flow of plenty."

Recently, I had this idea to get rid of more than half my posessions. I have too much stuff. Things I don't use, care for, or need. I'm creating waste too frequently and I don't want to find myself consuming for consumption sake. So while I'm not so focused on "Material wealth", I'm striving for spiritual wealth. I'm striving for empowerment and happiness through that spiritual wealth.

I've stopped saying things like "when I have 'x' I'll be happy." Because if I'm placing my complete happiess in something external, in something material then I don't think I'll ever experience true happiness.
