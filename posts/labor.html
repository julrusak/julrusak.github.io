Make Your Time Count
September 4, 2016

It's Labor Day, so I've been thinking hard about the work I put in and my desired outcome. I recently watched the episode of The Office where Jo, the owner of Sabre (recent buyer of Dunder Mifflin) tells Micheal that everyone can leave when they're satisfied with the work they've put in for the day. This inspired me to focus some attention on my own productivity.

Some days I only have a small window of time to hop on the mat, so I make that time count. If I have two hours to practice, I make that time count too, I would argue I make that time count even more. Ultimately, I do my best to be satisfied with the work I have put in, since my goal is progress. And that's such a simple goal. Just get 1% better everyday, this is so much easier than concrete goals in a set amount of time.

As for today, I'll keep it short as I'm off to enjoy a beautiful day with some beautiful people!
