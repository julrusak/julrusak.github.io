As an energetic artist and developer, I aspire to create visually dynamic and functional technologies. I enjoy both the collaborative process and working solo on projects and am enticed by large problems. I've learned to stop asking myself, "What do I want to do?" and start asking myself, "What problem do I want to solve?"
