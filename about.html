What is my story?

I grew up idolizing the cast members of Saturday Night Live, so I'm your typical funny gal. I moved to New York City at 21 to pursue a career on the stage. I had always loved the feeling of being on stage. I quickly found out that I do not love the feeling of being in an audition. I was well into my 20's before I understood that filmmaking is an art form, not just entertainment. I remember the moment I realized 'my parents were right all along' and subsequently realized 'adults have great advice!' Due to this realization, I will often stop and take in a moment, forcing my brain to remember each detail.
